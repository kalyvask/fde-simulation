---

## Notes on this draft

**Why this structure**: two-ledger (from me / from you) makes the contract visible to both sides. The "from you" half is the part rookies skip; making it visible is what makes the contract real.

**Why these dates**: deliverables you control, paced over two weeks. No outcome promises. No percentages.

**Why the adjuster quote is verbatim**: signals you listened and that you'll use her script, not yours.

**Why the EOD-tomorrow window**: gives her one chance to amend without forcing a follow-up exchange. After that, the contract is locked.

**What to avoid adding**: apologies, kill-criteria recaps (those live in the discovery memo), outcome numbers, pre-emptive wedge guesses.
