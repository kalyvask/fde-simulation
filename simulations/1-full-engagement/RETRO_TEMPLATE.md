# Engagement Retrospective — Template

> Fill this out at the end of week 4. Be specific. Be honest. Subtract 1 point per dimension from your gut score (graders are too generous to themselves).
>
> The retro is the single highest-leverage artifact for your next engagement — what's in it determines what you do differently next time. Don't speed-run it.

## Use

Copy this file into your work repo at the end of week 4. Fill it in over 1-2 hours. Then re-read it before starting any subsequent FDE engagement.

If you completed both Calder and Helix sequentially, fill out one retro per engagement, then a third "cross-engagement retro" that compares the two.
